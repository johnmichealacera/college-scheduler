# ClassSync — How to Use the Scheduler

A quick guide for teachers and staff on how to manage the class schedule.

---

## Getting Around

The menu on the left side has five sections:

- **Dashboard** — overview of today's classes and any scheduling issues
- **Teachers** — manage the list of teachers
- **Subjects** — manage the list of subjects
- **Rooms** — manage the list of classrooms
- **Schedule** — build and view the weekly timetable

---

## Step 1 — Add Teachers

Go to **Teachers** and click **Add Teacher**.  
Type the teacher's full name and click **Add Teacher**.

To update a name, click the pencil icon. To remove a teacher, click the trash icon.

---

## Step 2 — Add Subjects

Go to **Subjects** and click **Add Subject**.  
Enter the subject name (e.g. *Mathematics*, *Science*) and click **Add Subject**.

---

## Step 3 — Add Rooms

Go to **Rooms** and click **Add Room**.  
Enter the room name (e.g. *Room 101*, *Computer Lab*) and click **Add Room**.

---

## Step 4 — Add a Class to the Schedule

Go to **Schedule** and click **Add Class**.

Fill in the five fields:

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| Subject | Mathematics |
| Teacher | Dr. Jane Smith |
| Room | Room 101 |
| Day | Monday |
| Start & End Time | 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM |

Click **Add to Schedule**. The class will appear on the timetable immediately.

> **To edit a class** — click directly on the block in the timetable.  
> **To delete a class** — hover over the block and click the **×** that appears.

---

## Scheduling Conflicts

If you pick a time where the teacher or room is already taken, a **red warning** will appear before you can save. It will tell you exactly what the problem is.

Below the warning, **available time slots are shown as buttons** — click any one of them to automatically fill in a conflict-free time.

You cannot save a class until the conflict is resolved.

Conflicting entries on the timetable are shown with a **red highlight** so they are easy to spot.

---

## Filtering the Timetable

Above the timetable there is a filter bar. Use it to view the schedule for a specific teacher or room only. Click **Clear filters** to go back to the full view.

---

## Exporting to PDF

Click the **Export PDF** button at the top of the Schedule page to download the current timetable as a PDF report. If a filter is active, only the filtered classes will be included in the export.
